01 — Who I Am

A Little About Me.

I am Rashmi Sathish — a first-year undergraduate student pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science Core (CSC) at VIT Vellore. My academic focus centres on building robust, practical systems in algorithms, computer vision, and robotics, alongside a commitment to strong software engineering fundamentals. I am particularly motivated by the challenge of translating theoretical concepts into efficient, scalable, and production-ready solutions, grounded in strong software engineering fundamentals.

Through the Robovitcs Club (Junior Core Member, AI-ML Domain), I have extended my learning beyond the classroom — developing autonomous robots and AI-driven vision systems in a collaborative, hardware-focused environment.Working alongside senior engineers has strengthened my ability to approach complex problems systematically, contribute to end-to-end system development, and operate effectively in hardware-integrated, high-performance settings.

Since then, I have independently developed nine projects spanning computer vision, machine learning, NLP, and embedded systems.
